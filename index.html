Nisa Engineers is a premier AI and engineering consultancy headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Under the leadership of our Director and Lead Engineer, we've assembled a team of specialists who live at the intersection of research and real-world deployment.

Since 2021, we've tackled everything from custom large language model fine-tuning to IoT-based agricultural disease detection — solving problems that others call unsolvable. Our clients don't just receive deliverables; they receive working systems that keep working.

What sets us apart? We treat every project as a product. We obsess over architecture, sweat the edge cases, and communicate clearly from kickoff to launch. The 20+ consecutive five-star reviews spanning four years aren't a coincidence — they're a track record.
